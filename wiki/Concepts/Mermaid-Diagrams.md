# Diagrams (Mermaid)

This page documents how to include Mermaid diagrams in the CRML docs.

## Status

Mermaid rendering depends on MkDocs/MkDocs Material configuration. If diagrams do not render in the built site, confirm that Mermaid support is enabled in your MkDocs theme/extensions.
